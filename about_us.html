ABOUT US


Bethany School, is an unaided co-educational English medium, day school catering to students from Pre-School to High School, having earned the reputation within a short span of six years, as one of the best, and premier institutions in this 'City of Destiny' committed to imparting quality education. Outstanding Bethanians have won District, State, National and International acclaim in various disciplines, such as Roller Skating, Athletics, Dance, Dramatics and Films. Many foreign students have easily adjusted to the joyful learning atmosphere at Bethany School.


Specially designed for children, the individualized educational process at Bethany School is total. On the 18 of May, 1996, affiliation the school was recognised by the Government of Andhra Pradesh. The school obtained the "No Objection Certificate" for to the "Council for the ISC Examinations", New Delhi on June 25,1997. Numerous batches of ICSE (Class X) students have passed with flying colours since. Striving for excellence, building able leadership and citizenship, inculcating human values, developing character, motivating students to reach out with care and compassion for the under privileged in society, are concerns that are interwoven into the curriculum which is innovative and forward-looking. Students are developed to their highest potential. Bethany School is forging ahead in the field of education, carving historical milestones and investing in future generations who will determine the destiny of our nation and the world community.

With the growing number of children, the school opened its second campus and the Junior Section was inaugurated on July 23,1997. By the 5th of Feb, 1998, total affiliation to the ISC Board was achieved. And to meet further growing demand, the school expanded to a third campus and the Primary Section building was inaugurated on April 12th, 1999.

  Care for special children


Bethany School has a unique offer for children who are mentally and physically challenged. While each child receives training and education to suit his / her needs, interests and abilities, an opportunity is also provided to play and work alongside the other Bethany School students. The process of integration enriches the lives of all our children. A nominal fee is charged and the school subsidises this unit, which consists of about 30 students. You can partner with us and sponsor a child's education.



  Our vision

  • To acquire our own premises and to create the best infrastructure and facilities for continuing quality education in the heart of the "City of Destiny".
  • To establish training facilities for Staff and to develop improved, innovative methods of teaching-learning.
  • To facilitate exchange of students and Staff on an international basis.
  • To provide a Counselling Department for students.
  • To increase the number of free scholarships to students on the basis of merit cum means.
  • To computerise the Library.
  • To promote various club activities such as Interact, Literary, Science, Arts and Tourism.
  • To be actively involved in literacy and community development projects.
